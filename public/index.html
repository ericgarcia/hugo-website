Biography

Eric Garcia is a data scientist at Ptero and DataCowboys he is also a technology advisor / full stack developer at NuMundo. He is a creative artisan of machine learning algorithms and has designed and implemented many solutions for behavior analysis over the past few years. Prior to that he was a postdoc in the genome sciences department at the University of Washington and has repurposed many techniques from analyzing the human genome to analyzing user behavior. As a data scientist he is currently building algorithms in Scala and Apache Spark, but has extensive experience with the Python scikit-learn toolkit. As a full stack developer he architected the technology stack for NuMundo in Node.js and Angular. His passions include algorithms, permaculture, ecstatic dance, backpacking, and his long-term goal is to apply permaculture principles to grow his own food and live sustainably off the grid.
